About GoGo Ventures

GoGo Ventures is a DC-based promotions company founded and run by Joseph Jackson. We produce live GoGo music events, artist promotion, and community-focused experiences across the DMV. Our work includes band management, event production, social marketing and partnerships with local venues.

Our Mission

Keep the GoGo community growing by creating safe, accessible, and exciting events that spotlight local talent and build opportunities for artists and venues.

Featured Project — URB

URB — Urban Rhythm Band — led by Joseph Jackson, performs regularly in the region and is available for private shows, festivals, and club nights.
